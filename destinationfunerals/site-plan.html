Overview

Purpose

There are generally two times where the entire family gets together anymore, Weddings, and funerals. With families spread far and wide its hard to find the time together while taking care of the final arrangements when a loved one passes. This need not be the case, with Destination Funerals. When you navigate to our website you can choose the exotic locale to celebrate the life of your dearly departed, while we arrange all the service details. Heal together as you explore and bond as a family while celebrating the life of your loved one. I am a licensed Funeral Director with 8 years in the industry. I believe that true healing takes place when grief is shared. Let us bring you together and begin the healing process today.

Audience

My intended audience is families. It really encompasses everyone, but I feel the most important group it focuses on are the children. At a funeral it can be hard for children to mentally process a death, especially with parents and grandparents grieving and struggling through their own feelings there isn’t always space to help the littles understand. Destination funerals allows families to get together and vacation, while celebrating the life of their loved one. This allows for more time for littles to spend with the adults and hopefully opening those lines of communication.

Home page

“Grief shared is grief diminished” we are an organization that lives by this motto. Helping our customers diminish their grief through the shared process. We allow families the time it takes to spend together, talk about how they are feeling and process the changes that have come into their life. We focus on the one, which in turn focuses on the whole.

People will want to come visit my site because it will offer them an opportunity to not just attend a boring sad funeral. They will be able to get together with their loving family, celebrate the life of their departed love one and share the grief. This will appeal to people who dislike the old model of cookie cutter funerals. The future is here.

Our Story

My name is Sean Rood, I was first introduced to the field after High School. A friends family owned a mortuary and I was hired to help out with transfers, working services, cleaning the fleet, and building. Once that job ended and I moved on to college I didn’t put too much thought into the death industry until 2013 when my grandpa died. My entire family had planned on going out to Oregon for thanksgiving and my grandpa happened to pass the week prior. Since my entire family was already scheduled to be out there, we had all my dads siblings, and my cousins. We ended up spending time together at my grandma’s house, the beach, my aunts rental house, and had a large thanksgiving dinner together before having the funeral on Saturday. It was a sad time but also was fun spending time together and visiting. I hadn’t seen some of my family in years and it was nice catching up. Two things happened at this funeral, 1. I realized I wanted to be a funeral director, and 2. It planted a seed that would eventually become Destination Funerals. I am a licensed funeral director with 9 years in the funeral business. With the skills and knowledge, I have attained in my years of service I am qualified to guide your family through the funeral process as we also navigate the family through their grief. We also have talented travel agents on hand that know the industry and how to get use the best bang for our buck.

Destinations

Here are current destinations we offer services too. If you have a specific place you would like to go, please let us know. Hawaii- With services to Hawaii we have several islands to choose from. Have a memorial service at the base of Wailua falls, punalu’u beach with its beautiful black sand, or on one of the many old plantations that dot the islands. Or scatter your loved ones remains while sky diving over the crystal blue Pacific Ocean! California- Do you like the sand, or do you like the mountains? California has It all. With our California destination we can organize a beach side funeral or a mountain side retreat. Don’t forget the beautiful redwoods! Florida- visit beautiful St. Augustine, the oldest settlement in North America. Spend time with your family at Walt Disney World, and Universal Studios before having a peaceful beachside service. Cruise- Caribbean Cruises leaving out of Galveston Texas. Take your family on a 7-day cruise to the Caribbean, with stops at Playa del Carmen, Cozumel, Jamaica, and Barbados. We have many options of where we can setup and have a service, even at sea while sailing.
