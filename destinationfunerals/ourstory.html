My name is Sean Rood, I was first introduced to the field after High School. A friends family owned a mortuary and I was hired to help out with transfers, working services, cleaning the fleet, and building. Once that job ended and I moved on to college I didn’t put too much thought into the death industry until 2013 when my grandpa died. My entire family had planned on going out to Oregon for thanksgiving and my grandpa happened to pass the week prior. Since my entire family was already scheduled to be out there, we had all my dads siblings, and my cousins. We ended up spending time together at my grandma’s house, the beach, my aunts rental house, and had a large thanksgiving dinner together before having the funeral on Saturday. It was a sad time but also was fun spending time together and visiting. I hadn’t seen some of my family in years and it was nice catching up. Two things happened at this funeral,

1. I realized I wanted to be a funeral director.

2. It planted a seed that would eventually become Destination Funerals.

I am a licensed funeral director with 9 years in the funeral business. With the skills and knowledge, I have attained in my years of service I am qualified to guide your family through the funeral process as we also navigate the family through their grief. We also have talented travel agents on hand that know the industry and how to get use the best bang for our buck.

“I believe that true healing takes place when grief is shared.”
