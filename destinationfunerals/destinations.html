Here are current destinations we offer services too. If you have a specific place you would like to go, please let us know.

Hawaii

With services to Hawaii we have several islands to choose from. Have a memorial service at the base of Wailua falls, punalu’u beach with its beautiful black sand, or on one of the many old plantations that dot the islands. Or scatter your loved ones remains while sky diving over the crystal blue Pacific Ocean!

California

Do you like the sand, or do you like the mountains? California has It all. With our California destination we can organize a beach side funeral or a mountain side retreat. Don’t forget the beautiful redwoods!

Florida

Visit beautiful St. Augustine, the oldest settlement in North America. Spend time with your family at Walt Disney World, and Universal Studios before having a peaceful beachside service.

Cruises

Caribbean Cruises leaving out of Galveston Texas. Take your family on a 7-day cruise to the Caribbean, with stops at Playa del Carmen, Cozumel, Jamaica, and Barbados. We have many options of where we can setup and have a service, even at sea while sailing.
