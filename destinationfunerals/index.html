“Grief shared is grief diminished” we are an organization that lives by this motto. Helping our customers diminish their grief through the shared process. We allow families the time it takes to spend together, talk about how they are feeling and process the changes that have come into their life. We focus on the one, which in turn focuses on the whole.

There are generally two times where the entire family gets together anymore, Weddings, and funerals. With families spread far and wide its hard to find the time together while taking care of the final arrangements when a loved one passes.

This need not be the case, with Destination Funerals. As you navigate our website you can choose the exotic locale to celebrate the life of your dearly departed, while we arrange all the service details. Heal together as you explore and bond as a family while celebrating the life of your loved one.

Let us bring you together and begin the healing process today.

“I believe that true healing takes place when grief is shared.”
